PREPARE YOUR HAIR

Start with well-combed, clean, dry hair and your hair extensions ready to use. To make neat, even portions where you wish to braid, use a wide-tooth comb. If your scalp is sensitive or your skin is dry, dab a small amount of style gel or hair grease on it. Although optional, this step can enhance comfort.

BEGIN BRAIDING

Take a short section of hair. It will be split into three equal pieces. Right section should be crossed over middle section. The left section should then be crossed over the new middle section. As you proceed down the area, continue crossing the parts over one another and adding hair as necessary.

FINISHING TOUCHES

Make sure the braid is as nice and tight as the rest as you continue braiding until you reach the end of the section. For each area of hair you want to repeat this procedure. Try to maintain constant tension and equal parts spacing until your finally finished.

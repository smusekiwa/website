Recycle

We operate a business that turns used braids into high-quality, recyclable synthetic hair extensions. We make an effort to lessen the volume of plastic produced in Africa and dumped in uncontrolled landfills or oceans. Millions of pounds of garbage have been produced worldwide as a result of the mass production of plastic synthetic hair for hairstyles primarily worn by Black women. According to the United Nations Environment Program, Africa recycles only 4% of its waste, a number that must rise in order to improve the environment. The urgent necessity to address this unrecognised environmental justice issue and eliminate plastic synthetic hair waste gave rise to this business.
